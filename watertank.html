Executive Summary

This project requests funding for the construction and installation of a 500,000-gallon steel water storage tank to serve the St. James Parish Water Distribution #2 system. This critical infrastructure upgrade is SHOVEL READY and essential for maintaining compliance with state and federal drinking water regulations, ensuring public health safety, and providing resilience against severe weather events.

Project Drivers & Benefits

Regulatory Compliance & Public Health

Prevents violations of the Louisiana Safe Drinking Water Program. The additional capacity ensures adequate pressure during peak demand, preventing backflow contamination.

Disaster Resilience

Designed to withstand high wind loads. Provides a critical reservoir during power outages or storm damage, ensuring potable water access when external systems fail.

Operational Efficiency & Safety

Significantly increases water reserves for firefighting. Lowers energy consumption by reducing pump cycling and allows for maintenance redundancy without service interruption.
